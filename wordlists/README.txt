Entropy Lab Wordlists
====================

This directory contains the official EFF diceware wordlists used by
Entropy Lab for cryptographically secure passphrase generation.

Files:
- eff_large_wordlist.txt: EFF Long wordlist (7776 words, ~12.9 bits/word)

Source: Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)
URL: https://www.eff.org/dice
License: Creative Commons (see EFF website for details)

These wordlists are automatically installed with Entropy Lab to
/ext/apps/Tools/entropylab/ on your Flipper Zero.
