Entropy Lab Wordlists
====================

This directory contains official wordlists from multiple cryptographic
standards for secure passphrase generation.

Wordlists:
- eff_large_wordlist.txt: EFF wordlist (7776 words, ~12.925 bits/word)
- bip39_english.txt: BIP-39 Bitcoin wordlist (2048 words, ~11.0 bits/word)  
- slip39_english.txt: SLIP-39 Shamir wordlist (1024 words, ~10.0 bits/word)

Standards:
- EFF: Electronic Frontier Foundation diceware standard
- BIP-39: Bitcoin Improvement Proposal 39 (mnemonic seeds)
- SLIP-39: SatoshiLabs Improvement Proposal 39 (Shamir's Secret Sharing)

Sources:
- EFF: https://www.eff.org/dice
- BIP-39: https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0039.mediawiki
- SLIP-39: https://github.com/satoshilabs/slips/blob/master/slip-0039.md

These wordlists are automatically installed with Entropy Lab to
/ext/apps/Tools/entropylab/ on your Flipper Zero.
